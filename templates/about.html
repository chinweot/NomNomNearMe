About Us

Connecting students and community members with free food events.

Our Mission

At Nom Nom Near Me, we believe that good food brings people together. Our platform helps students and community members discover gatherings with free meals while creating opportunities to connect with like-minded people.

We partner with campus organizations and community groups to bring visibility to events that offer free food and foster community connections.
