About Us

Connecting students and community members with free food events.

Our Mission

At Nom Nom Near Me, we believe that affordable food and events brings people together. Our platform helps students and community members discover gatherings with free/cheap meals while creating opportunities to connect with like-minded people at social events.

We allow campus organizations and community groups bring visibility to events that offer food and foster community connections.
